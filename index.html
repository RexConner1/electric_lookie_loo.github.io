Electric Vehicle Data Visualization

Welcome to the Electric Vehicle Data Visualization project. In this presentation, you will explore:

  • The distribution of electric vehicles across various counties
  • Popular electric vehicle models and their types
  • Comparison of prices between electric and non-electric vehicles
  • Fuel and electricity prices over recent years

Each slide provides detailed visualizations and insights to help you understand the growing trend of electric vehicles.

Distribution of Electric Vehicles Across Counties

Electric vehicles (EVs) are becoming increasingly popular. This map shows the distribution of EVs across various counties. Each red dot represents a registered electric vehicle. The density of dots in certain areas indicates the popularity and adoption of EVs in those regions.

Popular Electric Vehicle Models and Types

This chart displays the most popular electric vehicle models and types. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) are fully electric with no gasoline engine, while Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) have both an electric motor and a gasoline engine. Let's see which types and models are leading the market.

Comparison of Electric and Non-Electric Vehicle Prices

How do the prices of electric vehicles compare to their non-electric counterparts? This chart compares the average prices of various car models, highlighting the cost differences between electric and gasoline-powered cars.

Fuel and Electricity Prices

The running cost of a vehicle is a crucial factor for many buyers. This chart compares the average prices of gasoline and electricity over time, providing insights into the cost-efficiency of electric vehicles compared to traditional gasoline vehicles.
